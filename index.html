About

Ben is a marketing/brand manager based in Los Angeles, California. He graduated from the University of Notre Dame with a Bachelors of Arts in Television and Media Studies. Passionate about education, corporate social responsibility, sustainable development, documentaries, traveling, current affairs, and common sense public policy.

Currently, he is the Marketing Manager on contract for RedHoop, an educational tech startup that aggregates online courses into a simple user interface. He is looking to join an incredible company as a full-time employee.

Though he's primarily a marketing professional, he codes during his free time - learning Ruby on Rails, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, and CoffeeScript. Otherwise, he spends his time reading, writing, looking for cool articles on Reddit, or playing basketball.
