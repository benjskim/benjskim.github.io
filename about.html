Bio

I'm a User Experience Designer based in Mountain View. Though I was born in South Korea, I consider myself lucky to call LA home for the past 15+ years, though I recently re-located to Silicon Valley to join the UXPin team. Thanks to the amazing teachers and mentors in my town, I was accepted to the University of Notre Dame, where I received a Bachelors of Arts in Media Studies. I'm currently pursuing a Master Degree in Human-Computer Interaction at DePaul University.

I believe in the power of good design. I also see UX as a platform that allows me to contribute in many areas I care about, such as education, financial literacy, and sustainability. Aside from UX, some of my other interests include: reading, traveling, and watching the Los Angeles Lakers.
