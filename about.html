About Ben.

"Dumbledore's Army"

Bio

Ben is a UX Designer based in Los Angeles. Though he was born in South Korea, he considers himself lucky to call LA his home for the past 15+ years. Thanks to the amazing teachers and mentors in his town, he was accepted to the University of Notre Dame, where he received a Bachelors of Arts in Media Studies.

When he was a marketer for an early-stage startup shortly after college, he discovered a particular interest for User Experience. That interest led him to DePaul University, where he's currently pursuing a Masters Degree in Human-Computer Interaction.

Ben believes in the power of good design. He also sees UX as a platform that allows him to contribute in many areas he cares about, such as education, financial literacy, and sustainability. Aside from UX, some of his other interests include: reading, traveling, watching the Lakers, and attempting to play the piano. On weekends, he moonlights as a College Prep Tutor with the Great Expectations Tutoring Company.
