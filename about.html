Bio

I'm a User Experience Designer based in Los Angeles. Though I was born in South Korea, I consider myself lucky to call LA home for the past 15+ years. Thanks to the amazing teachers and mentors in my town, I was accepted to the University of Notre Dame, where I received a Bachelors of Arts in Media Studies. I'm currently pursuing a Masters Degree in Human-Computer Interaction at DePaul University.

When I was a marketer for an early-stage startup after college, I discovered a particular interest for UX. I recognized UX as the combination of several key interests in my life: creating meaningful design, examining human behavior, and building better products.

I believe in the power of good design. I also see UX as a platform that allows me to contribute in many areas I care about, such as education, financial literacy, and sustainability. Aside from UX, some of my other interests include: reading, traveling, watching the Lakers, and attempting to play the piano. On weekends, I moonlight as a College Prep Tutor with the Great Expectations Tutoring Company.
