Overview

Upaji is/was a social dining web application connecting cooks with potential guests looking to experience an authentic dining experience. I was hired as their UX Design Intern in the summer of 2014 with my tasks ranging from redesigning their home page to critiquing user flows.

However, because I was the only UX person in the US team (while the rest of the tech team resided in India/Pakistan), progress was often impeded by communication hardships and vision differences with the Product Manager, a learning experience I will address in the "Other Challenges" section. Because the startup eventually folded later that summer, my internship lasted only 10 weeks. Overall, working at Upaji was a fantastic experience; I have nothing but positive memories of my time there.
